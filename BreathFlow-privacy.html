BreathFlow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

BreathFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a guided breathing exercise application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

BreathFlow does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or account credentials
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Health data
  • Financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or any other personal files

3. Data Stored on Your Device

BreathFlow stores the following data locally on your device only using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Breathing session records — technique used, duration, cycle count, and timestamp
  • App preferences — selected theme, default technique, session goal, haptic and reminder settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

BreathFlow does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services. The app operates entirely offline.

5. Network Usage

BreathFlow makes zero network requests. The app functions completely without an internet connection. No data is ever transmitted from your device.

6. Local Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reminder feature, BreathFlow uses iOS Local Notifications to send you reminders. This is processed entirely on-device by the iOS operating system. No notification data is sent to any server.

7. Data Security

All data is stored locally using Apple's built-in SwiftData framework and is protected by your device's native security features, including device passcode and encryption.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by BreathFlow, simply delete the app from your device. Since no data is stored externally, uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data.

9. Children's Privacy

BreathFlow does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
