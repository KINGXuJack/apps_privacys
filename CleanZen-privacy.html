Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

CleanZen is a fully offline cleaning puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. No account, login, or internet connection is required to play.

Data Collection

CleanZen does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • No device identifiers or tracking data
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Data Storage

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

CleanZen does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

CleanZen does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
