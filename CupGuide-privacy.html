Privacy Policy

CupGuide 2026: Fan City Guide — Last updated: June 18, 2026

Summary: CupGuide 2026 does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline. No account, no login, and no internet connection is required.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any data. CupGuide 2026 is designed to function completely offline. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No device identifiers are tracked (IDFA, IDFV, or advertising identifiers)
  • No location data is accessed or recorded
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are gathered
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies are used

2. On-Device Data

CupGuide 2026 stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults:

  • Your preferred appearance setting (light, dark, or system)
  • Teams you follow for personalized match filtering
  • Cities you mark as favorites
  • Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the app removes all stored data automatically.

3. Third-Party Services

CupGuide 2026 does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media frameworks. There are no embedded trackers of any kind.

4. Data Sharing

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to share. We do not sell, rent, or transfer any information to advertisers, data brokers, or any other entity.

5. Children's Privacy

CupGuide 2026 does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no user accounts, no data collection mechanisms, and no external links that could lead to data collection.

6. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored on our servers, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our end. All preferences stored locally on your device can be cleared by deleting the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be included in a future app update and posted at this same URL. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
