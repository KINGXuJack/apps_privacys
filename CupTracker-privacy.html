Cup Tracker 2026 — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Cup Tracker 2026 does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Cup Tracker 2026 ("the App") is a free, fan-made sports companion application for the 2026 soccer tournament. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles information when you use it.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal data. No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are used.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

3.1 Tournament Data (Pre-loaded)

Match schedules, team information, and tournament bracket structure are bundled within the App at installation. No network access required.

3.2 Live Score Data (Network Required)

Real-time scores are fetched from football-data.org. Only competition/match identifiers are sent — no personal information.

3.3 User-Generated Data (Stored Locally)

Match predictions, favorite matches/teams, friend list, and app settings are stored exclusively on your device using Core Data and UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

3.4 Calendar Events (Optional)

If enabled, the App creates calendar entries using Apple's EventKit. Calendar data is never read or transmitted.

3.5 Push Notifications (Optional)

Notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No data is sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

ServicePurposeData Transmitted
football-data.orgLive match scoresCompetition ID, match date range only

No analytics, crash reporting, advertising, social media, or payment services are used.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with anyone. No data is sold, shared with advertisers, or provided to any third party.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit personal data, risk is minimal. Local data is protected by iOS sandboxing and device encryption. Deleting the App removes all stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Rated 4+ on the App Store.

8. Your Rights

You can delete all App data by uninstalling. You can revoke notification or calendar permissions in iOS Settings at any time.
