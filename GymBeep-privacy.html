GymBeep Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026 · Last updated: May 21, 2026

GymBeep ("the App", "we") is an offline interval-training timer. This policy explains what information the App handles. The short version: GymBeep does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account or sign-up is required.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.
  • The App does not request access to your contacts, location, microphone input, camera, photos, calendar, or health data.
  • The App does not make any network requests. It operates entirely offline.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide its core features, GymBeep stores the following only on your device, never on a server:

  • Workout presets — built-in and user-created interval configurations (work / rest seconds, rounds).
  • Workout history — completed sessions (preset name, rounds, duration, completion timestamp) used by the History and Stats tabs.
  • Preferences — sound theme, volume, haptics on/off, screen-awake setting, prepare/countdown seconds.

This data is persisted via Apple's local SwiftData store and UserDefaults. It is included in your encrypted device backup if you have iCloud Backup or Finder/iTunes backup enabled — that backup is controlled by Apple, not by us.

3. Notifications & Audio

GymBeep can play audio cues during a workout and (optionally) post a local notification when a session completes. These run entirely on the device and are not transmitted anywhere. You can disable them in iOS Settings → Notifications → GymBeep at any time.

4. Children's Privacy

GymBeep is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Third-Party Services

GymBeep does not embed any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks. There are no third-party services with access to your data.

6. Your Rights

Because all data lives on your device, you have full control:

  • View — open the History and Stats tabs.
  • Delete — Settings → "Clear Workout History", or remove the App to wipe all data.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
