Privacy Policy — KhoomeiPath

Last updated: June 2, 2026

KhoomeiPath ("the App") is an overtone and Mongolian throat singing practice studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information. In short: the App collects no personal data and works entirely offline.

1. Information We Collect

KhoomeiPath does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

2. Microphone Access

The App requests microphone access solely to analyze your singing in real time and display a frequency spectrum of the overtones you produce. Audio is processed live on your device and is never recorded, saved, or transmitted anywhere. The microphone stream is discarded immediately after analysis.

3. Local Data Storage

Your practice logs, completed lessons, and settings are stored only on your device using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the App removes all of this data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, cloud servers, or external APIs. It functions completely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of exposure. All app data remains on your device under the protection of iOS.

7. Your Rights

You retain full control of your data. You can delete all locally stored information at any time by removing the App from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us
