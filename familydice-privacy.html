FamilyDice Fun - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

FamilyDice Fun is a fully offline family activity dice game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

FamilyDice Fun operates entirely offline. The app:

  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers
  • Does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • Does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device sensors

Local Data Storage

The app stores game data (player profiles, game history, favorite activities) locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time through the app's Settings
  • Is removed when you delete the app

Third-Party Services

FamilyDice Fun does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's Privacy

FamilyDice Fun is designed for family use and is safe for children of all ages. We do not collect any information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
