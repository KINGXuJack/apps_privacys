FamilyDice Fun - Support

About

FamilyDice Fun is a family bonding activity dice game with 46+ activities across 6 categories. Roll dice to discover fun challenges for the whole family.

FAQ

How do I start a game?

Open the app, go to the Play tab, add players (or use Quick Start), select a game mode, and roll the dice!

Can I play alone?

Yes! Select the "Solo" game mode to enjoy activities by yourself.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to any server.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings and tap "Clear All Game History". You can also delete the app to remove all data.

Does this app require internet?

No. FamilyDice Fun works 100% offline.
