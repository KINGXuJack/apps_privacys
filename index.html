FridayQuest Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

FridayQuest is a fully offline application. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Introduction

FridayQuest (Bundle ID: com.xiaoyu.FridayQuest) is developed by PittWong. This privacy policy explains how the app handles -- or more accurately, does not handle -- your personal information.

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. FridayQuest was designed from the ground up to operate entirely on your device with zero data collection.

Information Collection

FridayQuest does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected (name, email, phone number, or otherwise).
  • No usage or analytics data is gathered.
  • No device identifiers or fingerprints are recorded.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used.
  • No user accounts or login credentials are required.

The app stores quiz scores and saved activities locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data exists solely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Data Usage

Since FridayQuest does not collect any data, there is no data to use, process, or analyze. The locally stored information (such as quiz scores and saved activities) is used exclusively within the app on your device to provide its core functionality.

Network and Internet Access

FridayQuest is a 100% offline application. It makes no network requests of any kind. The app does not connect to the internet, does not communicate with any servers, and does not download or upload any data.

Third-Party Services

FridayQuest does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • No analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel).
  • No advertising networks or ad SDKs.
  • No crash reporting services.
  • No social media SDKs.
  • No third-party tracking tools of any kind.

Device Permissions

FridayQuest does not request access to any sensitive device features. It does not use:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Photo library
  • Notifications
  • Bluetooth or other hardware sensors

Data Security

All data generated by FridayQuest resides locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID as applicable. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data interception during transfer.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data (quiz scores and saved activities) will be permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

FridayQuest does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because the app is entirely offline and collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. No special parental consent is required since there is no data collection to consent to.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Since the app collects no data, any future updates to this policy will most likely be minor clarifications rather than material changes to data practices.
